# grounded-or-ask

- Statement: Every claim in a distilled or generated artifact is either cited to a source or raised as a question — a silent assumption (an inference dressed as fact, an uncited claim, a paraphrase that outruns what its source supports) is a defect. When a source is silent, the honest output is a question to the Gardener (LAW-10), never a confident guess.
- Rationale: The prime directive is to convert entropy into structure (SEED.md §0) — but structure laid on unverified inference is entropy wearing structure's clothes, and it compounds: the next agent trusts the false fact and builds on it. The dangerous failure mode when metabolizing the field (the intake family) or generating an artifact is the *plausible* one — a fluent paraphrase or a confident synthesis the source never actually backs. Forcing every claim to cite-or-ask makes that failure impossible to commit silently: an unsupported claim must announce itself as a question rather than harden into a fact nobody can trace back. This is the grilling protocol (SEED.md §5) raised into a standing law over knowledge, not only architecture.
- Enforcement: structural test — the generated-artifact family is bound by [validate-generated](../../.seed/checks/validate-generated.ts): a `docs/generated/` artifact must match its regeneration from named sources, so a hand-added, uncited claim cannot survive. The distilled-reference family (`docs/references/`) is bound by [validate-references](../../.seed/checks/validate-references.ts): every claim carries a citation, provenance carries a retrieval date and — for a repo GitHub can pin — a commit, the grounded/inference split (`**Seed reading:**`) is present, and where the cited corpus is saved in-repo a quoted span must appear in it and no source entry may be silently dropped ([plan 0004](../plans/active/0004-intake.md) scope item 3; ring [0024](../rings/0024-intake-network-free-metabolizer.md)). This file's own claim of enforcement — that it names a mechanism whose enforcer exists — is bound by [validate-principles](../../.seed/checks/validate-principles.ts). The residual the machinery cannot yet judge — whether a paraphrase stays *faithful* to its source ([E-013](../plans/entropy-ledger.md)) — is held doc-only for now: nothing is committed on the agent's authority; the Gardener ratifies (SEED.md §5, compose-not-commit).
- Exceptions: Structural and navigational prose that asserts nothing about the world — a heading, a cross-reference, an index line — makes no claim and needs no citation. Everything that asserts a fact about a source or the field does.
